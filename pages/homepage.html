Studio Policies

• 50% advance payment required to confirm booking
• Balance payment due on the day of shoot before session begins
• Accepted payment methods: Bank transfer, UPI, credit/debit cards
• All prices are in Indian Rupees (₹) and inclusive of GST
• Invoice provided upon request for business bookings

• Free cancellation or rescheduling 48 hours before booking
• 24-48 hours notice: 50% advance amount forfeited
• Less than 24 hours notice: Full advance amount forfeited
• Rescheduling subject to availability
• One free reschedule allowed per booking

• Maximum 10 people allowed in studio (including crew and talent)
• Setup and breakdown time included in booking duration
• Studio must be left in original condition
• No smoking, alcohol, or illegal substances permitted
• Damage to equipment or property will be charged at replacement cost
• Studio staff reserves right to terminate session for policy violations

• All included equipment covered under studio insurance
• Rental equipment requires separate damage deposit
• Client responsible for their own equipment and belongings
• The Face Craft Studio not liable for lost, stolen, or damaged personal items
• Professional liability insurance recommended for commercial shoots
• Equipment must be returned in working condition

• All content created remains property of the photographer/client

• Client responsible for obtaining model releases and permissions
